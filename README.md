# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (2020)

This project presents a detailed analysis of AutoCar LTD's sales performance between January and December 2020. It was built using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, and PowerPoint, and highlights key insights such as total sales, sales trends, customer demographics, top-performing branches, and car brands.

📊 Project Overview

- Company: AutoCar LTD  
- Time Period:January – December 2020  
- Total Sales: 1,491 vehicles  
- Average Buyer Age: 34.3 years  
- Top Sales Month: November  
- Lowest Sales Month: December  
- Most Active Branches: Lagos, Osun, Ogun  
- Main Customer Age Range: 26–35 years  
- Top-Selling Brands: Ford and Toyota

Key Insights

- 📈 Sales peaked in November (200 units) and dropped significantly in December.
- 🧑 Customers aged 26–35 accounted for the highest number of purchases (480).
- 🏢 Lagos branch led in sales, closely followed by Osun and Ogun.
- 🚙 Ford and Toyota were the most preferred brands.
- 📉 Ekiti branch had the least sales, indicating a need for market strategy review.

💡 Recommendations

- Improve operations and marketing in underperforming branches like Ekiti and Ondo.
- Target young professionals (26–35 years) with tailored campaigns.
- Focus inventory on top-performing brands and models.
- Prepare for peak sales in Q4, especially November.
- Investigate the sharp drop in December sales to prevent recurrence.
